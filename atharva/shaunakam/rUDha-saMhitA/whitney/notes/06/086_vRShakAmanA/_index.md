## Comment
Found also in Pāipp. xix. Employed by Kāuś. (59. 12), in a kāmya rite, by one who is vṛṣakāma (śrāiṣṭhyakāma, comm.); and the schol. (note to 140. 6) adds it to v. 3. 11 and vii. 86, 91 as used in the indramahotsava.
