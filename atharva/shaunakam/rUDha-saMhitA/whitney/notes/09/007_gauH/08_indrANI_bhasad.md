## Translation
8. Indrāṇī his buttock, Vāyu his tail (*púcha*), the purifying \[soma\]  
his whisk (*vā́lās*).

## Notes
Some of the mss. (Bp.²s.m.E.I.O.D.R.T.Kp.) read *bā́lāḥ*, which is  
preferable, since all the other passages have it. Ppp. omits, making our  
vs. 9 follow our 6.
