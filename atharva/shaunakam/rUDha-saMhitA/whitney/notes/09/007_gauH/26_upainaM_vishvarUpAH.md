## Translation
26. Cattle all-formed, of every form come unto (*upa-sthā*) him who  
knoweth thus.

## Notes
Ppp. reads *upāi ’naṁ rūpaṁṇvataṣ paśavaḥ* ⌊intending *rūpavantaṣ*⌋.  
  
⌊The quoted Anukr. says *ṣaḍviṅśo brāhmaṇo gavaḥ*. The *avasānarcas* are  
summed up as 26.⌋
