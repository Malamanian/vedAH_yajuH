## Translation
14. They that rush unto the heart, that stretch along the vertebrae—not  
injuring etc. etc.

## Notes
Prāt. iii. 47 prescribes the form *uparṣánti* (instead of *upārṣánti:*  
p. *upa॰ṛṣánti*) in this and the two following verses. Ppp. reads every  
time *upadiśanti*.
