## Translation
33. Uprooting when being turned about (? *pari-ā-kṛ*), destruction when  
turned about.

## Notes
The participles are rendered according to the Pet. Lexx. The Anukr.  
expects us to resolve *pari-ā-* once, but not both times. Bp. reads  
*-ākrīyá-*.
