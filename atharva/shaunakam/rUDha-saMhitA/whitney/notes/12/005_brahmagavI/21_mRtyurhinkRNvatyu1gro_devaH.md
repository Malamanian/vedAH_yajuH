## Translation
21. Death when uttering *hing;* the formidable god when slinging about  
her tail.

## Notes
All the *saṁhitā*-mss. read *-tyù3gró* ⌊K. *ū̀g-*⌋. This verse also has  
16 syllables, divisible into 8 + 8, but evidently only by accident.
