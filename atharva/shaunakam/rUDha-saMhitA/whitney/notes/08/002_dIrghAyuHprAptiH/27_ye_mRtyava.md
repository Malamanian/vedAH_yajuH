## Translation
27. The deaths that are a hundred and one, the perditions (*nāṣṭrā́*)  
that are to be over-passed—from that let the gods free thee, from Agni  
Vāiśvānara.

## Notes
Ppp. reads in **b** *nāṣṭrātta* (*-tu?*) *jīvyāḥ*. ⌊See note to iii. 11.  
5 for "101 deaths."⌋
