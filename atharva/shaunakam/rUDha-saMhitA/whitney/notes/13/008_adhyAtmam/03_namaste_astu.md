## Translation
48. Homage be to thee, O conspicuous one (*paśyata*); see ⌊*páśya*⌋ me,  
O conspicuous one.

## Notes
*Paśyata* is an anomalous and forced substitute for *darśata*, made in  
this passage only, for assonance with *paśya*. The Anukr. ratifies the  
combination *te astu*.
