## Translation
14. Increasing thee, O Indra, with worship (*bráhman*), the imploring  
seers have sat down \[for\] the session (*sattrá*).—Thine, O Vishṇu etc.  
etc.

## Notes

*śakvarī*. ⌊Verses 13 and 14, as was noted above, are wanting in Ppp.⌋
