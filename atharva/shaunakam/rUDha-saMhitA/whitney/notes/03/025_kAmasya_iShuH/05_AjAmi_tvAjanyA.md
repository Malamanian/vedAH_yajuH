## Translation
5. I goad thee hither with a goad (*ā́janī*), away from mother, likewise  
from father, that thou mayest be in my power (*krátu*), mayest come unto  
my intent.

## Notes
The second half-verse is identical with vi. 9. 2 **c, d**, and nearly so  
with i. 34. 2 **c, d**.
