## Translation
28. Three tongues glisten (*dīdī*) within the mouth of Varuṇa; of  
these, the one that shines (*rāj*) in the middle is the cow, hard of  
acceptance.

## Notes
The mss., as is usual in such cases, are divided between *duḥpr-* and  
*duṛpr-* in **d**.
