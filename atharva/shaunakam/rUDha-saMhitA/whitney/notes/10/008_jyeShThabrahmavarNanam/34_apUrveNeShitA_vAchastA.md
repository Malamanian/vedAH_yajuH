## Translation
34. Where both gods and men (*manuṣyà*) are set (*śritá*) as spokes in  
a nave—I ask thee of the flower (*púṣpa*) of the waters, where that by  
magic was placed.

## Notes
The Anukr. takes no notice of the redundant syllable ⌊perhaps the first  
*ca?*⌋ in **a**.
