## Translation
27. In whose member the thirty-three gods shared severally the limbs  
(*gā́tra*)—those thirty-three gods verily only (*éka*) the  
*bráhman*-knowers know.

## Notes
Or (so Muir), 'some *bráhman*-knowers.' Ppp. reads in **b** *gātrāṇi  
bhejire*.
