## Translation
10. Unharmed \[am\] I, with unharmed kine, long-lived, having all my  
men; let this amulet, the *varaṇá*, protect me, being such, from every  
quarter.

## Notes
Ppp. reads in **b** *-pāuruṣaḥ* (as usual, where *pūr-* and not *pur-*  
is meant).
