## Translation
12. I bear this *varaṇá* being long-lived, one of a hundred autumns; may  
it assign to me both kingdom and authority, to me cattle and force.

## Notes
Ppp., as noted above, reads for the second half of this verse our 11  
**c, d**, and *vice versa*.
