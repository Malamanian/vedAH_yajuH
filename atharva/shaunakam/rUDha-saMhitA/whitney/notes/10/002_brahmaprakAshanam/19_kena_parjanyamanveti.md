## Translation
19. With what does he go after Parjanya? with what \[after\] the  
out-looking Soma? with what \[after\] both sacrifice and faith? by whom  
was mind put in him?

## Notes
Ppp. reads *āpnoti* for *anv eti*, and has for **c, d** our 20 **c, d**  
(but *puruṣaḥ* in **c**).
