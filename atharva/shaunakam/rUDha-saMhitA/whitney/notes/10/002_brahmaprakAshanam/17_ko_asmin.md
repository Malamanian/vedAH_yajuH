## Translation
17. Who put in him seed, saying "let his line be extended"? who conveyed  
into him wisdom? who gave (*dhā*) \[him\] music? who dances?

## Notes
Ppp. has, for **a**, *ko ‘smin reto ‘dadhāt;* at end of **b**, *itaḥ;*  
for **d**, *ko vāśām ko anṛtaṁ dadhāu*.
