## Translation
10. Of Indra the bull (*vṛ́ṣan*), of king Varuṇa, of the Ādityas, of the  
Maruts, the spirit (*śárdhas*) \[is\] formidable; the noise of the  
great-minded, creation-stirring, conquering gods hath arisen.
