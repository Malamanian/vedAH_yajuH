## Translation
7. A golden ship, of golden tackle, moved about in the sky; there \[is\]  
the sight etc. etc.

## Notes
As to the correspondence and the extent of this verse, see the note to  
vs. 6. Ppp. reads *hiraṇyena nāur* ⌊and omits **c, d**⌋.
