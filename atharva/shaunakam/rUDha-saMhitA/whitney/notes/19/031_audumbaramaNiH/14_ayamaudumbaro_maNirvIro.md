## Translation
14. This amulet of *udumbára* is bound, a hero, to a hero; let it make  
for us a winning rich in honey, and may it confirm to us wealth having  
all heroes.

## Notes
Some of the mss. accent *sánim* in **c**. Ppp. reads *ucyate* for  
*badhyate* in **b**.
