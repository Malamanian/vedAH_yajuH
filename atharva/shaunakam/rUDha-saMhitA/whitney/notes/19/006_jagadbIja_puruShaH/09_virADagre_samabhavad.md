## Translation
9. Virāj in the beginning came into being (*sam-bhū*); out of Virāj,  
Purusha; it, when born, exceeded the earth behind, also in front.

## Notes

*purā*. ⌊For *puraḥ* in the Berlin ed., read *puráḥ*.⌋
