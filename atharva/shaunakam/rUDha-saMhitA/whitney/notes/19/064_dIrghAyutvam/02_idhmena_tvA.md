## Translation
2. With firing, O Jātavedas, with fuel do we increase thee; so do thou  
increase us, both with progeny and with riches.

## Notes

see Kāuś. 57. 26 note⌋ the further pāda *dīrgham āyuḥ kṛṇotu me*, and
