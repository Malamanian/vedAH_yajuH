## Comment
The hymn is found also in Pāipp. iii. As noted under the preceding, it belongs to the abhaya gaṇa; and the comm. points out sundry uses of the gaṇa (śānti K. 16; Nakṣ. K. 18; Pariśiṣṭa 5. 3).
