## Translation
2. By Soma are the Ādityas strong; by Soma is the earth great; likewise  
in the lap of these asterisms is Soma placed (*ā-dhā*).

## Notes
  
  
  
  
  
Is RV. X. 85. 2, without variant. ⌊Cf. MP. i. 9. 2; Wint., p. 74; MGS.  
i. 14. 8 and p. 157.⌋
