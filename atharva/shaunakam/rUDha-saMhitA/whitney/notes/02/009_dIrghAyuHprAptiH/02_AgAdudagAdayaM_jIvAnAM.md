## Translation
2. This man hath come, hath arisen, hath gone unto the troop (*vrā́ta*)  
of the living; he hath become of sons the father and of men (*nṛ́*) the  
most fortunate.

## Notes
Ppp. has in **c** *abhūta* (for *abhūd u*), and in **d** *nṛṇām.*  
⌊Pronounce *ā́* *agād*.⌋
