## Translation
3. From the sky \[is\] the root stretched down, from off the earth  
stretched up; with this, thousand-jointed (*-kā́ṇḍa*), do thou protect us  
about on all sides.

## Notes
Compare xix. 32.3, where *darbha*-grass is the plant simllarly described  
and used.
