## Translation
21. Let their hearts burst open (*ut-kas*), their breath pass up aloft;  
let dryness of mouth follow after our enemies, ⌊and⌋ not those who are  
friendly.

## Notes
The comm. renders *ut kasantu* by *śarīrād udgacchantu*, and *ud īṣatu*  
equivalently.
