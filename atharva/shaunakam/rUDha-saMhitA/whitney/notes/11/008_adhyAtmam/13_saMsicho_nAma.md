## Translation
13. Pourers-together namely are those gods who brought together the  
bringings-together; having poured together the whole mortal, the gods  
entered man.

## Notes
Ppp. reads *śaṅsatas* for *saṁsicas* in **a**, and *saṁsṛjya* for  
*saṁsicya* in **c**.
