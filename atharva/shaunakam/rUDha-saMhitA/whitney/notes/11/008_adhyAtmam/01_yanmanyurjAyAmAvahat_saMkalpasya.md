## Translation
1. When fury (*manyú*) brought his wife away from the house of  
contrivance (*saṁkalpá*), who were the groomsmen (*jánya*)? who the  
wooers (*vará*)? who also was chief wooer?

## Notes
Ppp. combines in **c** *kā ”san*. ⌊Its **c, d** = our 6 **c, d**.⌋
