## Translation
6. ⌊16.⌋ For him, from the upward quarter, ⌊17.⌋ they made the two cool  
months guardians, and heaven and Āditya attendants. ⌊18.⌋ The two cool  
months guard from the upward quarter, and heaven and Āditya attend, him  
who knoweth thus.
