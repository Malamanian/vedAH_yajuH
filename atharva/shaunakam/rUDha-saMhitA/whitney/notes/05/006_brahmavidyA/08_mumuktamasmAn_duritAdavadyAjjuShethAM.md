## Translation
8. Do ye (two) release us from difficulty, from reproach (*avadyá*);  
enjoy ye the offering; put in us immortality (*amṛ́ta*).

## Notes
Some of the mss. (Bp.²O.) read *asmā́t* instead of *asmā́n*. Ppp. has  
*asmāt*, and after it *gṛbhīthāt*.
